Mostly Asked Questions

What is required for Lip-syncing animation?

You merely need an audio file and a written script of the same audio file in order to get a job lip-syncing. Your text file needs to include all of the words and sounds that are contained in the audio file in order to ensure that the timing of the syllables is accurate.

Is there time limit for the Lip-syncing job?

You are not charged a dime for uploading any audio file, no matter how long it is or how many hours it contains. However, in order to make the lip sync task more manageable and to maintain the precision of the timing of the syllables, it is recommended to upload the job in small chunks.

Can I add my own lips assets?

For mouth components that have a transparent backdrop, you can utilize the png file type, having dimensions that are about equivalent to 375 x 154.

Can I use any language?

There is currently just support for the English language. As a result, you will only be able to upload English audio for the job of lip-syncing. Should you choose to upload any other language; the website will not function correctly.

What Video format will it make?

This tool will generate a mouth movement with a green backdrop, which you may remove using any video editing software, such as the VSDC video editing software.

How much free entries?

Multiple daily entries can now be made absolutely free for marketing purposes. This, however, may alter in the future subject to the price of website upkeep.

Will it save my time?

Producing a good length of lip sync animation from scratch and fine-tuning each frame by hand would take hundreds of hours. Our lip sync software, however, makes the process quick and easy. The only time-consuming part is creating the unique mouth elements, but if you use our ready-made mouth components, you'll be done in no time.

What are Comparative advantage of using lip syncing over manually adjusting mouth for each frame?

In order to speed up the animation creation process, animators would often complete all of the body motion first, leaving the mouth area vacant so that they may fill it with lip motion according to phonemes/phonics. As a result, there is now a dearth of animation; but, using this programme, animators and YouTubers can create mouth sync animation in a matter of minutes, relieving some of the pressure they've been under.
