About us

There is a wide variety of lip sync software that can be found on the internet nowadays, and each one comes with its own set of benefits and drawbacks. Some are free, some need payment, while others provide both options. It is essential to look for a lip sync tool that is appropriate for the kind of animation that you are working on at the moment.

MouthLipSync is an animation platform designed for professionals. The Lip Sync Tool is an excellent way to improve one's lip sync skills as well as to amuse one's friends and family.

This tool is only one of several tools that will be available in the future from our company for the creation of animations.

Our Vision

Our company motto is if a process is repetitive, it is preferable for it to be automated since it may help us save time, keep focused and organized. This tool gives you access to a selection of readymade mouth assets from which to choose the one that works best with your character. After you have chosen the mouth asset, you can then allow the automated tool do the task of mouth synchronizing for you. Should you so wish, you are also able to submit your own own mouth assets.
